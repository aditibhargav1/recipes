Burfi

Ingredient

  • Sugar
  • bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Crush the bread slices into fine crumbs.
  2. Mix the crumbs with a bit of milk to form a smooth dough.
  3. Cook the mixture in a pan with sugar until it thickens.
  4. Spread the mixture, let it cool, and cut it into burfi pieces.
